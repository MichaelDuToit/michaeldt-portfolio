PaceBud is Pace Calculator for runners built as a Progressive Web App. It can calculate either time, distance or pace when provided with inputs of the other two fields (ie to calculate time you need to provide input data to the distance and pace fields).

Being a Progressive Web App, PaceBud can be installed to your device and work offline.
